BAYVIEW PENTHOUSE

The Bayview Penthouse is a 3-storeyed family home that offers 360° panoramic views of the bay, city skyline and lush landscapes. The design derives inspiration from its surrounding context, creating an ever-evolving sensory experience that blends sophisticated aesthetics with natural serenity.

Influenced by the golden design eras of Art Deco & Mid- Century modern, the design palette and textures are delicately interwoven to create a signature charm.

Vast floor-to-ceiling windows and seamless access to large open-air terraces prioritize a unique sense of ‘indoor- outdoor living’ within the urban context. The floor configuration and spatial planning is meticulously crafted to suit individual and collective requirements of the family, deriving a sense of spatial warmth, exclusivity and tranquility.
