MODERN SERENITY

‘Modern Serenity’ is a minimalist three-bedroom private residence created by combining two separate apartment units into one spacious home. Designed for a technocrat client with specific requirements, the fusion of both apartments result in a large floor plate that offers a canvas for a well-balanced living experience.

The home features a spacious living room with a sun-deck, a fully-equipped modern kitchen with a separate dining area & family bedrooms. The planning caters to modern professional needs, with an ensuite master bedroom that includes an independent home office.

The minimalist design approach aims to create a clean and clear aesthetic that promotes a sense of calm and relaxation. Monochromatic themes emphasize the simplicity and elegance of the spaces while pops of color are strategically incorporated to enhance visual interest and a sense of personality.
