PALM WOODS PENTHOUSE

‘Palm Woods Penthouse’ is a luxurious 4-bedroom family home spread over 4500 square feet. The project offers expansive views of the surrounding bay and city skyline. Designed with a focus on accommodating the strong individual requirements of our clients, the project showcases a versatile layout plan and a timeless neoclassical design theme.

The layout and planning are interwoven to maximize natural sunlight and wind, creating a seamless connection between the levels of the penthouse and outdoor spaces. The use of fine natural materials such as marble, veneer and natural wood emphasize on exuding elegance and tastefulness, while the iconic design style features symmetrical lines, balanced proportions and refined elements across the project canvas.

With a wide range of amenities and thoughtfully detailed spaces, the design enables a modern haven of comfort and style.
