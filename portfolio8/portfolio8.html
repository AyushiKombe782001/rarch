FLAMINGO PENTHOUSE

The ‘Flamingo Penthouse’ is a two-storeyed, 5- bedroom penthouse. The 5000 square foot home, equipped with large open terraces on both floors, offers wide-ranging panoramic views of the surrounding flamingo sanctuaries, harbour and pristine wetlands.

Designed for three generations of a family, the project enables the creation of spacious living areas, private retreats and vital collaborative spaces to accommodate and stimulate the diverse needs of all family members.

An artfully crafted Gurudwara located at the center of the top floor reflects the spiritual beliefs of the family and promotes a sense of peace.

The design theme blends contemporary and traditional elements to create spaces that exude effortless vitality, refinement and finesse.
