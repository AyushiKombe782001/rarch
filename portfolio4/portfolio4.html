URBAN OASIS

The three-story penthouse, surrounded by its natural environment, serves as the inspiration for the theme of "Underlying Elegance" and the concept of bringing the outside in. The design focuses on preserving natural light while incorporating modern aesthetics that exude luxury and refinemen.

The floor configuration is carefully planned to optimize the functionality and flow of the penthouse. The main floor houses the common areas, such as the informal living area, dining room, kitchen, prayer area, and guest bedrooms. This layout allows family members to engage in various activities individually while still fostering a sense of togetherness within the same space.
