URBAN OASIS

‘Urban Oasis’ is a three-bedroom private residence that embodies the essence of bespoke luxury with versatile and contemporary aesthetics. The multi-functional layout and intrinsic space planning offers our clients the freedom and flexibility to customize spaces based on their preference, catering to their professional, recreational & social requirements.

The home features a central social area that serves as the core of the layout. The warm and inviting space connects the dining area, open-kitchen & allied bedrooms, fostering a sense of togetherness and enabling user privacy and independence when required. The design incorporates the use of concealed sliding door systems that facilitate flexible space addition. Exquisite natural material finishes, sleek French windows, warm color schemes and custom-built furniture encompass a trademark and timeless design.

Each space is meticulously designed to create a distinct atmosphere that reflects the personal styles and preferences of individual family members.
