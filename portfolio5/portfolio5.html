TROPICAL DEW VILLA

‘Tropical Dew Villa’ is an exquisite weekend home located amidst the pristine beauty of nature in Karjat. Spread across 10,000 Sq.ft and equipped with mesmerizing views of the Sahyadri Mountains and surrounding vistas, the villa offers a tranquil weekend getaway for our clients.

The landscape design aims to enhance the tropical aura and create a flourishing oasis within the built space. The careful selection of indigenous shrubs and plants add depth and diversity to the landscape, ensuring a vibrant and refreshing environment. A lush lawn at the front allows users to enjoy the expansive greenery and a kitchen garden in the backyard enables home-grown vegetables and fruits, promoting sustainable living.

The eclectic main entrance creates a sense of intrigue as guests approach. The design enables the creation of a large patio area with a modern roof, ideal for outdoor gatherings and relaxation. The exterior features sleek slate textures and carefully crafted metal trellises, adding a touch of sophistication to the villa. The interior design focuses on creating an inviting ambiance with large ceiling heights, enabling the villa to exude a sense of spaciousness and warmth.
