ABOUT US


R ARCH is a multifaceted architecture firm based in Navi Mumbai, India. With over a decade of dynamic industry experience, R ARCH has delivered projects across the design & construction spectrum ranging from luxury homes, residential buildings, offices & cultural establishments.

At R ARCH, we create spaces that reflect our clients vision while seamlessly integrating our expertise and creative flair. Committed to the spirit of consistency and timely delivery, we prioritize innovation, effective communication and comprehensive management of all our projects.

Our core values focus on translating ideas into reality with a keen eye for detail & an innate ability to transform spaces into captivating visual narratives that evoke a sense of refinement and sophistication.
